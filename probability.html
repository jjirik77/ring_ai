In this simulation, you'll see how AI models handle uncertainty. The grid on the right represents 50 items (emails or patients). Each has a hidden True State and a Probability Score from the AI.

Understanding Uncertainty

Machine Learning models rarely answer with a simple "Yes" or "No". Instead, they calculate a Probability Score. It is up to human designers to decide where to draw the line.

The Threshold

You will set the Decision Threshold. Any item scoring above your line is flagged. Set it too low, you get false alarms. Set it too high, you miss targets.

The Overlap

Models aren't perfect. Safe items sometimes look suspicious, and dangerous items sometimes look safe. This mathematical overlap guarantees mistakes.
