Core Target Architectures
Large Language Model (LLM) ▾
Uses self-attention Transformer networks to process colossal text corpuses, calculating the mathematical probabilities of next word chunks (tokens) in a sequence.
Computer Vision (CV) ▾
Employs deep layers (CNNs / ViTs) to inspect, classify, trace, and extract numeric structural vectors straight from raw image or video matrices.
Recommendation Engine (REC) ▾
Applies collaborative filtering to map clicking sequences, purchase patterns, and engagement metadata, predicting high-affinity future targets.
Multimodal AI (MULTI) ▾
Houses joint vector encoders to link text, audio, and visual nodes directly into one unified spatial reasoning dimension for cross-domain generation.
Natural Language Processing (NLP) ▾
Performs parsing, sentiment tracking, phonetic breakdowns, speech translation, and command intent extraction from natural written or spoken queries.
Primary Data Channels
• Text [text]: Linguistic characters, paragraphs, prompt commands, search queries.
• Pixel [img]: Still photograph rasters, live feeds, graphical frames.
• Behavioral [beh]: Dwell metadata, skips, click paths, view historical timestamps.
• Audio [audio]: Vocal frequencies, noise waveforms, acoustic signals.
• Sensor [sensor]: Continuous space telemetry, GPS, accelerometers, proximity fields.
